Accessibility

For my undergraduate capstone project, my team and I made a mobile-responsive webpage that supported live transcription for an educational setting. The vision involved a professor's words being transcribed in real-time on all students' mobile devices. When researching our project idea, we realized how much of the population suffers from hearing impairments. While our initial intention was to create something that was interesting, it opened my eyes to the realm of accessible technologies (and software). My interests, for this class, lie in creating similar accessible software that can help change the world for good.
